Forgiving Zone-Based Drag
Nonlinear Browser

How it works

Cross-space move Drag any tab over a space name — the whole row lights up. Drop to move the tab there as a top-level tab. No precision needed.
Reorder within space Drop anywhere on a tab row — snaps above or below based on midpoint. Always inserts as sibling, never child.
No nesting via drag Children are created via right-click → Move under. Keeps drag simple and predictable.
