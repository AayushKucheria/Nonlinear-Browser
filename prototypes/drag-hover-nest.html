Hover-to-Nest Drag
Nonlinear Browser

How it works

Default: sibling Drop anywhere on a tab row → inserts as sibling above or below. No precision needed. Above/below determined by midpoint only.
Hover 600ms → nest Hold still over a tab for 600ms. It glows purple. Release to make it a child. Move away to cancel.
Cross-space Drag over a space name — whole header highlights blue. Drop = top of that space.
